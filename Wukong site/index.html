Black Myth: Wukong

The Destiny of the Destined — Action RPG

Black Myth: Wukong is an action RPG by Game Science based on Journey to the West. Players play as the Destined One, a Monkey King reborn, on a journey through mythological worlds.

Released August 2024, it sold over 10 million copies in 3 days.

About the Game

What is it?

Black Myth: Wukong is a single-player action RPG inspired by Chinese mythology. Players take on the role of the Destined One, a successor to Sun Wukong. The game features fast-paced combat, deep boss encounters, and stunning environments.
